FAQ

Does UnMeet listen to my meetings?

Never. We don't access your microphone, camera, or meeting content. We only detect that a meeting tab is open — by URL pattern matching. No audio, no video, no screen recording.

How is the cost calculated?

You set your hourly rate (or we estimate one based on your role). Meeting cost = duration × participants × avg hourly rate. You control the numbers.

Who sees my data?

You. Only you. All tracking happens locally in your browser. Cloud sync is optional and end-to-end encrypted. Your boss cannot access your dashboard.

Which meeting platforms do you support?

Google Meet, Zoom Web, Microsoft Teams, and anything that opens in a browser tab. Desktop app support coming soon.
